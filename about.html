about me.


Living with passion is what I strictly adhere to. "Always do what you love" no matter how cheezy this sounds it holds absolutely true. That's why I enjoy designing (and web development). Not for the sole reason of "loving" to create but it helps me get rid of the world and escape into a different dimention of my own. I relish the process of starting from idea to a final product. The art of creating is something I am always fascinated by.

I got into web development in 2017 when I was studying End User Computing and also IT Technical Support A+. That's when I got introduced to web development and I havent looked back since. The web is so massive and there's so much that can be done, I joined Umuzi so I can learn more about web development and one day become a full stack web developer.

I am a continous learner. Learning for me equates to exploring new ideas and growing as an individual. I just cannot imagine my life devoid of designing and art. I freely axpress my creativity and imagination by designing and creating art. And to give my passion of designing and art a platform I have an Instagram account called, @steve_art16.
